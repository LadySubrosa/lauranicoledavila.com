Greetings!

Hi! My name is Laura! I am a creative and tech-oriented professional living in Austin, TX. I love all things art and design related, but also have a passion for logic and computer programming. As I keep myself in constant student mode, my curiosity and drive helped me succeed in my academic career and continue to help me learn new things as a young professional. In my spare time I enjoy drawing, experimenting with new vegan recipes, practicing guitar, and caring for my bunny, Lulu.

These days I am serving as a Software Engineer II for HEB Digital. I've got experience working closely with other designers, content writers, and developers to create customer-facing digital experiences for a variety of organizations and industries. I've done a little bit of everything related to web including user experience design, mock-up creation, and front-end development.

Since my true love has always been cartoons, I also do freelance animation, illustration, and graphic design. My most recent animation gig began in 2014 for OnStory, an award-winning television series produced by the Austin Film Festival. Check your local listings to watch on PBS, or check out the archive of episodes online.

If you're interested to see more samples of my work, collaborating or commissioning me, don't be shy!
